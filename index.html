About

JemsPantry is a smart pantry management app designed to help you organize your kitchen, reduce food waste, and save time and money.

Features

Track items & expiry dates
Reduce food waste
Manage shopping lists
100% local privacy - no data uploaded
Search and filter items easily
Smart categorization

Privacy

All your pantry data is stored locally on your device. Nothing is uploaded to external servers. Your data, your control.
