Privacy Policy for JemsPantry Effective date: 7 March 2026 JemsPantry respects your privacy. Information JemsPantry collects JemsPantry does not require you to create an account. The app stores the information you enter, such as: pantry items quantities locations storage types expiry dates notes shopping list items selected region How your information is used The information you enter is used only to help the app function, including: showing your pantry inventory managing your shopping list saving your app settings keeping your data available on your device Where your data is stored JemsPantry stores your data locally on your device. You are responsible for keeping your device secure. Changes to this Privacy Policy This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time.
