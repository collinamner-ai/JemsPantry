Privacy Policy for JemsPantry

Effective date: 7 March 2026

JemsPantry respects your privacy.

Information JemsPantry collects

JemsPantry does not require you to create an account.

The app stores the information you enter, such as:

  • Pantry items
  • Quantities
  • Locations
  • Storage types
  • Expiry dates
  • Notes
  • Shopping list items
  • Selected region

How your information is used

The information you enter is used only to help the app function, including:

  • Showing your pantry inventory
  • Managing your shopping list
  • Saving your app settings
  • Keeping your data available on your device

Where your data is stored

JemsPantry stores your data locally on your device.

Your data is not uploaded to a server by JemsPantry.

Data sharing

JemsPantry does not sell, share, or transfer your personal data to third parties.

Children’s privacy

JemsPantry is not designed to collect personal information from children.

Data deletion

You can delete your data at any time by:

  • Removing items inside the app
  • Clearing the app’s storage on your device
  • Uninstalling the app

Security

JemsPantry stores data locally on your device, but no method of storage is completely secure. You are responsible for keeping your device secure.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the updated policy within the app listing or app materials.
